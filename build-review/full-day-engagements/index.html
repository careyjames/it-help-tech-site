Full-Day & Extended Engagements

For projects that require sustained attention, primary-source technical research, deep analysis, and uninterrupted execution, we offer full-day and extended-day engagements.

These engagements reserve our time exclusively for your project. No other client work is scheduled during this period.

This allows for a fundamentally different level of service:

  • Continuous execution without interruption
  • Primary-source technical research, validation, and synthesis
  • System-level thinking across the full environment
  • Immediate iteration and decision-making
  • Full metacognitive focus on your infrastructure and problem space

Pricing

Full-Day Engagement (8 Hours)

$1,900 flat rate

Includes up to 8 hours of dedicated, uninterrupted service within a single day.

Extended Day (Up to 12 Hours)

$2,900 flat rate

Includes up to 12 hours of continuous, dedicated service.

Additional Time

Time beyond 12 hours is billed at the standard hourly rate ($275/hour), in 1-minute increments.

How This Works

Full-day and extended-day engagements are capacity reservations, not hourly bundles.

  • The full day is reserved exclusively for your project
  • No other client work is scheduled during this time
  • The flat rate applies regardless of actual hours used

This ensures complete focus, predictable pricing, and no billing ambiguity.

Why This Model Exists

Standard consulting models often involve multiple clients across a day, which works well for ongoing support and responsive service.

Full-day engagements are offered for cases where a single problem, system, or research objective benefits from sustained continuity and uninterrupted focus.

Instead of working around a project across multiple sessions, we work inside it continuously — analyzing, researching, building, troubleshooting, and refining in real time.

This approach produces:

  • Faster resolution of complex issues
  • Higher-quality system and architecture decisions
  • Reduced total project time
  • Fewer follow-up sessions
  • More rigorous research and validation of technical choices

Pricing Philosophy

Full-day pricing reflects a deliberate alignment of incentives:

  • You receive uninterrupted access and priority execution
  • We gain scheduling certainty and eliminate fragmented workload overhead

This allows for a more efficient and effective engagement on both sides.

The 8-hour engagement represents the optimal working window for sustained technical performance and deep research, and is priced accordingly. Extended-day engagements are available when required but are structured to maintain service quality and system integrity.

No upselling. No hidden incentives. No time inflation. Just dedicated work, clearly scoped and transparently billed.

Multi-Day Engagements

For large-scale deployments, primary-source research initiatives, rebuilds, or complex environments, multi-day engagements are available.

These are structured as consecutive full-day or extended-day reservations.

  • Scope and duration are defined in advance
  • Scheduling is reserved across all days
  • Pricing is based on the selected daily engagement tier

Multi-day work maintains continuity, eliminates ramp-up loss between sessions, and enables full-system execution and research without interruption.

When to Use Full-Day Engagements

Full-day or extended engagements are recommended for:

  • Network design and full infrastructure builds
  • Multi-system integration (servers, storage, endpoints)
  • Large-scale troubleshooting and stabilization
  • Security audits and remediation
  • Data migration and recovery coordination
  • Primary-source technical research and validation
  • Architecture planning and technology selection
  • Code integration, iteration, and system refinement
  • High-priority or time-sensitive environments

Final Notes

Full-day engagements are designed for clients who value:

  • Depth over fragmentation
  • Speed through continuity
  • Precision through sustained focus

This is not a discounted version of hourly work. It is a different class of engagement.
