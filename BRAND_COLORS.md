## Brand Rules (Copy-Ready Notes)
1. Keep blue as primary identity; logo blues are the strongest signal.
2. Use red only for the plus symbol.
3. Use gold as restrained utility accent (logo perimeter, hero pill border, gold CTA).
4. Keep Schedule CTA one blue-step below logo luminance.
5. Keep `san diego` subordinate steel-blue gray, never brighter than logo text.
6. Avoid introducing ad hoc colors without updating `STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
7. For Apple/Safari stability, keep logo text as solid fill + stroke (no transparent gradient text fill).
